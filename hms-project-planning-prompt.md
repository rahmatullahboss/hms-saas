# HMS (Hospital Management System) - Project Planning Prompt

## Project Overview
Create a comprehensive HMS software for a medical clinic/hospital with 4 distinct login portals.

---

## Login Portals (4 Types)

### 1. Laboratory Login
- View and print patient tests only
- No editing capabilities

### 2. Reception Login
- Patient registration and management
- Billing and payments
- Income/Expense tracking
- Investment management
- Bill settlement (Current, Due, Fire Service)

### 3. Pharmacy Login
- Medicine inventory management
- Total investment tracking
- Total income tracking

### 4. Managing Director & Director Login
- Daily income reports
- Daily expense reports
- Staff salary management
- Profit/sharing calculation

## Shareholder & Profit System

### Share Structure
- **1 Share = 100,000 Taka**
- **Total Shares = 300**
- **Profit Distribution = 100 People** (from 300 shares)
- **Owner Shares = 200** (for management)

### Monthly Calculation
- Total Income - Total Expenses = Profit
- Profit distribution per share (30% to profit holders, 70% to owners)
